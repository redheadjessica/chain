## The draft
A little heads-up that will save you a trip: please do not shave to tidy up before your first appointment.

We understand the instinct. You want to arrive neat and clean. But sugaring needs the hair to be about the length of a grain of rice, which is roughly two weeks of growth. Shaving a day or two before resets that clock, and we end up rescheduling or only doing part of what you came in for.

So skip the pre-visit shave. Come in a little fuzzier than feels natural. That is exactly what we want, and it is what lets each pull be cleaner and the result last.

Grow it out, and let us do the rest. 🌱
