# Consultation notes (anonymized, working notes)

- So many first-timers arrive having shaved two days before "to tidy up." Have to
  reschedule or do a partial. Need a clearer pre-visit note about growth length.
- Retinol / tretinoin users keep forgetting to pause it. A few near-lifts on the
  upper lip. Worth a standard heads-up in the booking confirmation.
- Brides keep booking a single appointment the week of the wedding. The good outcome
  needs a two- or three-visit runway so skin is calm and hair is on the right cycle.
- A lot of clients think sugaring and waxing are the same thing with a nicer name. They
  don't know the paste is water-soluble and only grabs hair. Once explained, they relax.
- New: switched to a firmer paste for humid summer days. Cleaner pulls, fewer breaks.

## Seasonal
- Spring: pre-sandals leg season rush. Book earlier.
- Winter: brows and face stay steady; legs slow down.
