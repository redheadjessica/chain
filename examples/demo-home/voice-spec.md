# Voice Spec — Alex Rivera (demo persona)

Seeded from `canon/voice-spec.template.md`. A `voice_spec` reference — it can live
here in chain_home, or point at a style file you already keep.

## Register
Plain-spoken and specific. Short sentences next to long ones. One clear point of view
per piece, backed by a concrete example. Warm but not soft; confident without
credential-listing.

## Hard rules (also enforced by lint_draft.py)
- Contractions by default.
- No em dashes, no semicolons.
- Say the point once; no "not just X but Y" scaffolding.
- No AI-fingerprint vocabulary.

## Truth discipline
Every claim traces to real work or existing writing. Uncertainties go to the packet's
"Questions for you", never into hedged prose.
