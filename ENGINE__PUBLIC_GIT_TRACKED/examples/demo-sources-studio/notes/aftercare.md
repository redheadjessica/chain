# Aftercare guide (given to clients)

For 24-48 hours after your appointment:
- Skip hot baths, saunas, and heavy workouts. Warm, open pores plus friction is how
  bumps start.
- No retinol, exfoliating acids, or self-tanner on the area for two days.
- Start gentle exfoliation on day three, then two or three times a week. This is the
  single biggest thing that prevents ingrown hairs.
- Wear something loose home. Tight denim right after is not your friend.

Rebook around four to six weeks, when regrowth is back to about a grain of rice. Staying
on a rhythm is what makes each session easier than the last.
