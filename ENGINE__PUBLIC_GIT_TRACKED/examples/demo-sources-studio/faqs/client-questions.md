# Frequently asked questions (Sweet Fern Sugaring Studio)

**Does sugaring hurt less than waxing?**
For most clients, yes. Sugar paste only grips the hair, not the live skin, so it pulls
less. First-timers are usually surprised how much calmer it feels than the waxing they
remember.

**Is it really all-natural?**
Our paste is sugar, lemon, and water. Nothing else. Clients with sensitive skin who
react to wax resins often do fine with sugar.

**How long does the hair need to be?**
About a grain of rice — roughly two weeks of growth. People come in too short all the
time because they shaved "to clean up" first. That actually resets the clock.

**Will the hair grow back thinner?**
Over many sessions the regrowth often comes in finer and patchier, because the follicle
is disturbed gently and repeatedly. It is not instant and we never promise permanence.

**Can I come in on my period, or if I'm pregnant?**
Yes to both, with small adjustments. Skin can be more sensitive around your cycle.
