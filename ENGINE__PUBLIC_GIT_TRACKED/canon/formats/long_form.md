# Format: long_form

A long-form piece that develops one argument fully. This single format covers the whole
range from a long social post to a full essay — there is no separate "article" format;
it is all `long_form`. The **channel** decides the destination (Medium, Substack, a
website, LinkedIn Articles, or another configured place).

## Mechanical (linted)
- Roughly 400–2,500 words (the brief may set a `target_length`).
- An intro and a close are present.
- Headings / sections are allowed (the channel pack may encourage or discourage them).

## Judgment (writer + evaluator, not linted)
- Develops one real argument with concrete examples; earns its length.
- Opens with substance, closes without a tidy-bow summary.
- Stands fully on its own, even when a companion piece exists.

## Bundles
A long_form piece may be produced with a `companion_post` (see that pack). They share one
`idea_id`; the companion takes an angle and never summarizes the long-form piece.
