# Format: short_form

A short, single-idea piece — a social post, a quick note. Channel-neutral in shape; the
channel pack (linkedin / neutral / …) governs the mechanics. `companion_post` is a
specialized short_form (see its pack).

## Mechanical (linted)
- Short: one idea, roughly 40–300 words.
- An opening line is present (fold-aware on LinkedIn).
- No required sections beyond the body.

## Judgment (writer + evaluator, not linted)
- One observation, cleanly made. Cut anything that isn't the point.
- The opening should earn the next line. (Strength is the evaluator's call, not the lint's.)
