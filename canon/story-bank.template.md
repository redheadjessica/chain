# Story Bank (template)

Reusable TRUE stories with their facts pinned down, so no agent ever invents,
embellishes, or composites. Writers may only use stories from this bank (or from
your corpus); anything else goes to the packet as a question, never into prose.

> Replace the placeholder entries with 3-5 stories you already tell out loud.
