## Guidance for CHAIN

Discover ideas across the whole corpus rather than filling categories. A compelling
story, observation, or lesson beats topping up an underrepresented theme. The goal is
a body of work that feels cohesive and unmistakably one person's — not a balanced
editorial spreadsheet.
