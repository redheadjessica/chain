# Channel: Neutral

The lowest-common-denominator pack, selected by `channel: neutral` — used when an
idea is developed before a channel is chosen, or for a website essay with no
platform-specific mechanics.

## Mechanical (linted)

- No repeated punctuation.
- Length governed by the format pack only.
- Links allowed inline; anchor text in natural words.

## Judgment (writer + evaluator, not linted)

- Write it well and channel-agnostic; a later pass can re-target it to LinkedIn,
  Medium, or Substack by swapping the channel pack, not the idea.
