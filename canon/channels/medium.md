# Channel: Medium

Selected by a brief with `channel: medium`. For long-form essays and articles.

## Mechanical (linted)

- Length: typically 800–2,500 words for an article (format pack sets the target).
- A title and an optional subtitle/dek are expected.
- Inline links are fine and encouraged (unlike LinkedIn) — link quietly, anchor text
  in natural words.
- Section structure permitted (headings, lists) per the format pack.
- No repeated punctuation.

## Judgment (writer + evaluator, not linted)

- Earn the length: an article should develop a real argument, not pad a post.
- Open with substance, not throat-clearing.
- Concrete examples over abstraction; one clear thesis carried through.
- If a companion post exists, the article stands fully on its own.
