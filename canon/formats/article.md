# Format: article

A long-form essay. Default channel: Medium (or Substack / website).

## Mechanical (linted)
- Roughly 800–2,500 words (brief may set a target_length).
- A title; optional subtitle/dek.
- Sections/headings allowed; intro and close present.

## Judgment
- Develops one real argument with concrete examples; earns its length.
- Stands fully on its own, even when a companion post exists.
- Opens with substance, closes without a tidy-bow summary.

## Bundles
An article may be produced with a `companion_post` (see that pack). They share one
`idea_id`; the companion takes an angle and never summarizes the article.
