# Format: short_post

A single-idea social post. Default channel: LinkedIn (but channel-neutral in shape).

## Mechanical (linted)
- Short: one idea, roughly 60–250 words.
- Opening line present (fold-aware on LinkedIn).
- No required sections beyond the body.

## Judgment
- One observation, cleanly made. Cut anything that isn't the point.
- A short post can stand alone or serve as a **companion** to a long-form piece —
  when it's a companion, see `companion_post.md` for the angle rules.
