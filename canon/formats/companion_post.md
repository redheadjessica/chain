# Format: companion_post

A short post that accompanies a long-form piece (usually LinkedIn → Medium). It
shares the long-form piece's `idea_id` and links to it via `parent_piece_id` +
`relation_type: companion-of`.

## The rule: a companion is NOT a summary

Its brief carries a `companion_angle`, one of:
- one compelling **observation** from the long-form piece
- the **origin story** (why you started writing it)
- the central **tension** the long-form piece resolves
- one concrete **example** from the long-form piece
- **why** the long-form piece was written
- a **related but distinct** angle the long-form piece doesn't fully cover

## Mechanical (linted)
- short_form length and channel mechanics apply (default LinkedIn).
- If the long-form piece link is included, it goes in the first comment, not the body.

## Judgment (evaluator, incl. bundle mode)
- Does it create genuine interest in the long-form piece?
- Does it also stand on its own for a reader who never clicks?
- Is it unnecessarily repetitive with the long-form piece?
- Does each piece fit its own channel?
